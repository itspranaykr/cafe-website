Americano

Caffè americano, also known as americano or American, is a type of coffee drink prepared by diluting an espresso shot with hot water at a 1:3 to 1:4 ratio, resulting in a drink that retains the complex flavors of espresso, but in a lighter way

Long BLack

A long black is a coffee drink made by pouring hot water into a cup and then adding a shot or two of espresso. It's a common drink in Australia and New Zealand.

Order Now

caffe macchiato

Caffè macchiato is an espresso drink with a small amount of steamed or foamed milk. The word "macchiato" is Italian for "stained" or "marked".

Order Now

latte

A latte macchiato is an Italian coffee drink made by pouring espresso into steamed milk. The name translates to "stained milk".

Order Now

Flat white

A flat white is a blend of microfoamed milk poured over a single or double shot of espresso. This microfoam is steamed milk infused with air which creates a smooth, velvety texture and creamy taste. Precise pouring and steaming of the microfoam is essential when making a flat white.

Order Now

Mocha

Although a mocha is often interpreted differently across the world, the basis is that a shot of espresso is combined with chocolate powder or syrup, followed by milk or cream. It is a variant of a latte, in the sense that it is often 1/3 espresso and 2/3 steamed milk
